Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 2026
Data Collection
canBet reads your Canvas LMS name, user ID, course enrollment, and assignment submission data from ohio.instructure.com using your existing authenticated browser session.
No login credentials are ever accessed or stored.
Data Storage & Transmission
All data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.
No data is transmitted to any external server.
No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.
Data Use
Data is used solely to calculate your in-app Bit balance within the canBet extension.
Data Deletion
To delete your data, uninstall the extension or clear extension storage in your browser settings.

Questions or concerns canbet.business@outlook.com
