# canBet – Productivity Lootbox System
# Vision / Problem Statement
Traditional learning platforms track grades, but canBet tracks effort and consistency.
- Encourages task completion
- Gamifies academic progress
- Creates friendly competition through leaderboards
- Increases engagement with coursework
---
# How does CanBet work?
## Bits
There are two main ways to earn bits:
  - Bits can be earned by submitting assignments on time.
  - Bits can be earned through the daily login system.

## Crates
Crates are what you spend bits on, upon opening a crate you will get a themed item.

## Items
Items come in various rarities, your goal is to get the rarest items and flex on your friends!

## Leaderboard
Compete with other CanBet users to see who can get the rarest items, most bits, and open the most crates!
